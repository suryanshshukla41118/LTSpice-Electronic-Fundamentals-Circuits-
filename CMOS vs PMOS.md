# CMOS vs PMOS
In this, I have implemented simple Circuit of XOR and XNOR gate using PMOS and CMOS and compared its power usage w.r.t each other.
The PMOS circuit uses Resistor Transistor Logic when leads to more power dissipation = 320.69uW while the CMOS uses very less power = 16 uW which is about 20 times
 less. CMOS may have less poweer consumption but it takes more Area to implement.
 NOTE: If we replace PMOS with NMOS the circuits will become complimentary of what it was. Suppose it is XOR with PMOS, if we replace with NMOS it will become XNOR and
 vice-versa. The result for power will be similar to its complimentary counterpart.
